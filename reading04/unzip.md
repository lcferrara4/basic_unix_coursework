TLDR - unzip
==========

Overview
--------

unzip will list and extract compressed files

Examples
--------

- Unzip the archive: unzip archive.zip

- List files in zip: unzip -l archive.zip

- Unzip archives in path: unzip archive.zip -d /this/path
